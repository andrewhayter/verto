## Meta-Strategy: The "What Is It Costing You?" Series

Group these under a single branded series across FB/Pinterest:

> **"I calculated mine. Here's what I found."**

This phrase is engineered for reshares — personal, curiosity-driven, implies a revelation without spoiling the number. Output cards are Pinterest-ready (before/after cost comparisons).

Series candidates:
- Credit score → loan costs (calc #1)
- Weight loss spend → GLP-1 cost (calc #2)
- Menopause symptom management → HRT cost (calc #3)
- Hair loss products → telehealth cost (calc #4)
- Vaping spend → annual + health cost (calc #13)
- Chronic pain treatments → what it's cost over time (calc #17)
