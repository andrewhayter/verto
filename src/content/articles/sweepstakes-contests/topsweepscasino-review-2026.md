## What a Sweepstakes Casino Actually Is

A sweepstakes casino is not an online casino. It does not accept real-money bets and is not licensed under gambling regulations. It operates under a different legal framework — one that has existed in U.S. law since the 1950s.

1. Players receive Gold Coins (free, no cash value) and Sweeps Coins (prize currency, redeemable for cash)
