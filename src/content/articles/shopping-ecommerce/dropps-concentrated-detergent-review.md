## What Dropps Is and How It Works

Dropps is a concentrated laundry detergent delivered in a dissolvable polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) pod. PVA is water-soluble and biodegrades within 28 days in standard wastewater treatment conditions — it is not plastic in any functional sense, though it is technically a synthetic polymer.

Each pod contains a concentrated surfactant formula without the water dilution that makes liquid detergents heavy to ship. The packaging is cardboard with no plastic liner. The subscription model ships pods quarterly, reducing per-order packaging further.

Dropps makes four laundry product lines:
- **Laundry Pods — Stain & Odor:** The flagship formula, suited for everyday laundry
- **Sensitive Skin:** Fragrance-free, dye-free, formulated for eczema and skin sensitivity
- **Oxi Booster Pods:** Oxygen bleach additive for whites and heavily stained items, used alongside the main pods
- **Fabric Softener Pods:** Dryer-safe formula that replaces liquid fabric softener
