## The 30-Day Protocol

**Days 1–7:** 10-minute sessions with Standard Formula, once daily. Sensitivity onset around day 3 — cold water produced brief sharp pain on two lower front teeth. This is normal hydrogen peroxide sensitivity and is temporary, but it is uncomfortable.

**Days 8–14:** Switched to Sensitive Formula for one week to allow enamel to stabilize. Sensitivity resolved by day 10. Shade continued to improve at a slower rate.

**Days 15–30:** Alternated Standard and Sensitive formulas based on sensitivity level. Final shade assessment at day 30.

**Total serum used:** Approximately 70% of one Standard wand and 50% of the Sensitive wand. The included serums are sufficient for a full 21-day protocol (Snow's recommended initial course) with some remaining.
