## App and Features: What Actually Works

**Scheduling:** The most useful feature. I have Pura running 7–9am at intensity 5 (light morning fragrance), off during the day, 5–10pm at intensity 7 (dinner and evening). This happens automatically every day without any action from me.

**Dual vial:** The device holds two vials and can alternate between them on a schedule or manual toggle. I run a "day" scent and an "evening" scent — the app switches between them. For seasonal rotation, this is useful: one winter and one fresh scent, toggling based on mood.

**Intensity control:** The 1–10 scale genuinely matters. At 8–10, Pura is overpowering in a standard bedroom. At 3–5, it's ambient. The ability to dial in exactly how strong the fragrance is solves the "this candle is too strong in a small room" problem.

**App stability:** I've had one connectivity drop in 4 months — the device went offline and required a reset. Resolved in 5 minutes. Otherwise stable.
