## How I Used It: Stacking on Top of Existing Cashback

My existing cashback setup before adding SocialGood: Discover It card (rotating 5% categories) + Rakuten for certain retailers (additional 1–5% as Amazon credit or PayPal cash).

SocialGood stacks. When I shop Amazon through SocialGood and pay with my Discover It card:
1. Credit card cashback: 1–5% USD (depending on whether Amazon is in the quarterly 5% category)
2. Rakuten cashback: up to 3% depending on current offer
3. SocialGood SG tokens: 2–5% in SG token value at current price

The maximum theoretical stack at the best conditions: 13%+ total return on Amazon purchases. In practice, rotating category caps ($1,500 quarterly) and varying Rakuten rates mean the effective stack is 4–8% most of the time.

SocialGood's contribution: a few percent of every Amazon purchase in crypto, accumulated passively on purchases I was making anyway.

### Can you really earn crypto just from regular shopping?

Yes — SocialGood (and similar platforms like Lolli for Bitcoin rewards) pay cryptocurrency rewards when you shop through their portal links, similar to how traditional cashback portals work. The crypto component adds speculative upside (SG token appreciation) over standard cashback, at the cost of simplicity and predictable USD value. For purchases you'd make anyway and already have a credit card and cashback portal setup, SocialGood adds marginal extra value with no out-of-pocket cost.

## The Honest Caveats

**Token price volatility:** My SG accumulation was worth $33 when I checked. It was worth $89 when SG ran up in Q4 2025. It was worth $12 in a period of token price decline. Crypto cashback is not predictable dollar cashback.

**Conversion complexity:** To realize USD from SG tokens requires: withdraw from SocialGood to crypto wallet → deposit to exchange → sell → withdraw to bank. Takes 30–60 minutes of setup for a first-time crypto user; 10 minutes for subsequent cashouts. Not difficult, but more friction than Rakuten's PayPal payment.

**Tax:** In the US, crypto received as rewards may be taxable income at fair market value when received. Consult a tax professional for treatment of crypto rewards.

**Who should use it:** Existing crypto users who want crypto exposure on everyday spending at no extra cost. Anyone building a multi-layer cashback stack. Not recommended as the primary or sole cashback mechanism for users who want simple USD payouts.
