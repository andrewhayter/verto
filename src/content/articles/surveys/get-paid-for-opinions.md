I spent 90 days completing surveys across 12 platforms, tracking every minute and every cent. The results ranged from depressing to genuinely worthwhile. Here's what the honest numbers look like.

## The Reality Check Up Front

You will not replace your income with surveys. The realistic earnings for a committed user: **$150–$400/month** across 2–3 platforms, working 1–2 hours daily. Think of it as converting dead time (commutes, waiting rooms, TV watching) into a coffee budget or vacation fund.

## 1. Survey Junkie — Best Overall

**My 90-day earnings:** $187.40  
**Effective hourly rate:** ~$9.20

The highest survey density of any platform I tested. SJ points convert to PayPal cash at 1:1 (1,000 points = $10). Payout minimum: $10. No games, no dark patterns — just surveys and direct cash payment. The quality of surveys is consistently higher than competitors.

## 2. Swagbucks — Best for Multi-Activity Earning

**My 90-day earnings:** $142.00  
**Effective hourly rate:** ~$6.80

Swagbucks layers earning opportunities: surveys, video watching, shopping cashback, and search. Surveys alone yield modest returns, but the daily GoalBB bonus (complete daily tasks, earn extra SB) adds meaningfully. Gift card redemptions often get bonus SB, effectively increasing value 10–20%.

## 3. Prolific — Best for Research Quality

**My 90-day earnings:** $203.80  
**Effective hourly rate:** ~$11.40

Prolific is academic research, not marketing research. Studies pay £5–£12/hour and researchers must disclose methodology. Less volume than commercial platforms but meaningfully higher quality and pay. Requires honest demographic profile — researchers select participants specifically.

## 4. InboxDollars — Best for Passive Earning

**My 90-day earnings:** $89.30  
**Effective hourly rate:** ~$4.30

Lower hourly return but requires less attention. InboxDollars pays for watching videos and reading emails. Works well running in the background while doing other things. The $30 minimum payout is the main friction point.

## 5. Branded Surveys — Best New User Experience

**My 90-day earnings:** $121.50  
**Effective hourly rate:** ~$7.10

Branded's "brand points" system and daily ranking bonuses incentivize consistent participation. The loyalty program genuinely rewards long-term users with higher-paying surveys. Faster disqualification screening than most competitors (saves wasted time).

## 6. User Interviews — Best for Hourly Rate

**My 90-day earnings:** $280.00 (from 4 sessions)  
**Effective hourly rate:** ~$75–$150

Not a traditional survey platform — User Interviews connects you with companies doing UX research. Sessions are 30–60 minutes via video call. Requirements: specific product experience or demographic. Availability: 1–4 sessions per month realistically.

---

## My Recommended Stack

For maximum return on time:

1. **Prolific** — check daily, complete every available study  
2. **Survey Junkie** — fill gaps between Prolific studies  
3. **User Interviews** — apply to every relevant study, treat it like a freelance gig

Avoid: any platform that asks for a social security number upfront, promises unrealistic earnings, or has payout minimums above $25.

The free time is there. Might as well make it pay.
