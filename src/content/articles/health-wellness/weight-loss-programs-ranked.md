Weight loss is simultaneously one of the most personal and most universally confusing topics in health. The market is flooded with programs making 90-day transformation promises that vanish like morning fog.

We evaluated eight programs against three criteria: clinical evidence, real user outcomes at 6 months, and sustainability. Here's the unfiltered breakdown.

## 1. Medical GLP-1 Programs (WeightWatchers Clinic, Noom Med, Found)

Since semaglutide went mainstream, medically supervised GLP-1 programs have become the category leader in clinical outcomes. Average weight loss of 15–22% of body weight at 12 months dwarfs every diet-only approach. The platforms now pair medication with behavioral coaching.

**Avg. 12-month loss:** 15–22% body weight  
**Monthly cost:** $200–$400 (insurance coverage varies widely)  
**Best for:** BMI 30+ or BMI 27+ with weight-related conditions  
**Reality check:** Side effects are real; medication is not a substitute for lifestyle change

## 2. Noom (App Only) — Best for Psychology-Based Approach

Noom treats overeating as a cognitive-behavioral problem, not a willpower problem. Short daily lessons, food logging with color-coded categories, and human coaching at higher tiers. Weight loss of 5–8% at 6 months — modest but sustainable.

**Avg. 6-month loss:** 5–8% body weight  
**Monthly cost:** $59–$199  
**Best for:** People who've failed traditional diets and need to rewire habits

## 3. WeightWatchers (Points System) — Best Community Accountability

WW's 60-year track record includes more peer-reviewed studies than any other commercial program. The community aspect drives adherence. Users who attend weekly meetings lose 2–3x more than app-only users.

**Avg. 6-month loss:** 5–7% body weight  
**Monthly cost:** $23–$55  
**Best for:** People who thrive with community accountability

## 4. Calibrate — Best for Metabolic Health Focus

Calibrate pairs GLP-1 medication with a year-long curriculum on food, sleep, exercise, and emotional health. The emphasis on improving metabolic markers, not just the scale, appeals to those who want comprehensive health improvement.

**Avg. 12-month loss:** 10–15% body weight  
**Monthly cost:** $150 (medication billed separately)  
**Best for:** Those wanting a complete metabolic health overhaul

## 5. OPTAVIA — Best Structured Meal Replacement Plan

OPTAVIA provides pre-portioned "fuelings" plus one "lean and green" meal daily. Removes decision fatigue entirely. Average loss of 12 lbs in 12 weeks for completers, though the direct-sales model is a turnoff for some.

**Avg. 12-week loss:** 12 lbs  
**Monthly cost:** $350–$500 (includes food)  
**Best for:** People who want full structure without thinking about food

---

## The Honest Bottom Line

No program works without sustained behavior change. The best program is the one you'll follow for 12+ months. If you have significant weight to lose and no contraindications, speak with your doctor about GLP-1 options — the clinical evidence gap between medication and diet-only approaches is now very large.

Use this comparison to understand your options. Then talk to a medical professional.
