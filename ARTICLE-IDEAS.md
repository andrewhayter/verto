> Version: 2026-06-11
> Status: [ ] = Not started | [x] = Done | [~] = In progress

## How to use this document
Each article has:
- **Format tag** — the editorial frame that makes it non-shilly
- **Hook** — the one thing that earns the reader's first paragraph
- **Angle** — what makes this *interesting* beyond "this product exists"
- **CTA offer** — the primary MaxBounty offer it converts to
- **Slug** — where it lives in `/src/content/articles/`

Batches are sized for 4–6 articles each. Write one batch at a time, build, ship, move to next.

---

## FORMAT GLOSSARY
- `[MATH]` — Run actual numbers. Not "affordable." "$179/month vs $1,200."
- `[COMPARE]` — I tested/researched 4–6 options. Here's the ranking with honest trade-offs.
- `[NARRATIVE]` — First-person or strong second-person. A real-feeling story arc.
- `[CONTRARIAN]` — Challenge the obvious advice. Earn the twist.
- `[EXPLAINER]` — Teach me something I didn't know. Offer is the natural next step.
- `[LISTICLE]` — Ranked list with genuine evaluation criteria. Not a dump.
- `[REVIEW]` — Single product, first-person test, honest about what didn't work.
